KobuBox

A cozy, hackable, screen-lite storyteller box for families — currently living in the land of prototypes, napkin sketches, and late-night firmware experiments.

What exists today? Read the dream

Vaporware, but with strong cozy energy.

Where this is right now

KobuBox is not a product you can buy. It’s an open hardware side project that’s still taking shape.

Today, it’s mostly:

  • An ESP32 dev board on the bench
  • Early firmware experiments in Rust
  • A barcode/QR scanner and e-paper display being brought to life
  • First integrations with Home Assistant and Sonos

If you like following along with early, slightly-chaotic projects, you’re in the right place.

What KobuBox wants to be

The short version:

  • A toy-like box kids can trust and understand
  • Printable cards instead of proprietary plastic figures
  • Local-first tech that plays nicely with the stuff you already own
